*Scary Shawarma Kiosk: the ANOMALY* is a Roblox game where you work at a humble shawarma stand. Your job isn't just serving customers and making shawarma, but interacting with them. However, not all customers are human. In *Scary Shawarma Kiosk: the ANOMALY*, you'll encounter various monsters and strange occurrences, such as headless men, smiling men, bizarre Santas, and sometimes even your own kebab will attack you.

So, obviously, to survive in the game, you need to be aware of all sorts of anomalies. This includes unusual customers, strange events, and jump scares. You also need to remember that you can't miss serving any normal customers. This Roblox guide will provide you with all the information you need. In this guide, you'll find information to help you complete the game.

## All Endings Guide

When you start your shift at Scary Shawarma Kiosk: the ANOMALY, your fate is not yet sealed. Each shift has five possible endings, and this guide lists all five endings to help you better understand the possible outcomes.

Remember, every choice you make in the game can affect the ending. You'll need to make judgments about which customers to serve, which ones to refuse service to, and how to handle unexpected events. Fortunately, this guide will help you understand these possibilities in advance.

| Ending Name | Unlock Conditions | Description |
| ----------- | ----------------- | ----------- |
| [**Ending 1: Perfect**](#first-ending-the-perfect-ending) | **Zero mistakes** during entire shift | Perfect completion |
| [**Ending 2: Unstable**](#second-ending-an-unstable-ending) | **Fewer than 5 mistakes** | Standard completion |
| [**Ending 3: Silent**](#third-ending-the-silent-end) | **Open** the envelope from Smiling Man | Transported to alternate dimension and devoured |
| [**Ending 4: Erased**](#fourth-ending-the-erased-ending) | **Look back** at booth during 2 AM inspection | Transported to conveyor belt and fall into meat grinder |
| [**Ending 5: Neutralized**](#fifth-ending-neutralization-ending) | **5 or more mistakes** | Killed by Inspector emerging from floor |

### First ending, the perfect ending.

To achieve the perfect ending in Scary Shawarma Kiosk: the ANOMALY, you must make zero mistakes during your shift. This means you must strictly follow the rules and correctly identify every anomaly you encounter. This might sound simple, but in reality, you will encounter a large number of anomalies to identify throughout your shift, which is not an easy task, as everyone makes mistakes. However, it's not impossible; once you master all the rules of the game, you can successfully complete your shift and unlock the perfect ending.

![Ending 1 Perfect - completing shift with zero mistakes](/Scary-Shawarma-Kiosk/Scary_Shawarma_Kiosk_Perfect_Ending.webp)

### Second ending, an unstable ending.

The "Unstable Ending" is the second-best ending and one of the most common, as making one or two mistakes is quite common. However, you need to make fewer than five mistakes to unlock this ending. If you miss one or two anomalies, refuse to serve a genuine customer, or miss a certain event, this ending will be triggered at the end of your shift. You must make **fewer than five** mistakes, as more than five mistakes will unlock a different ending.

![Ending 2 Unstable - completing shift with fewer than 5 mistakes](/Scary-Shawarma-Kiosk/Scary_Shawarma_Kiosk_Unstable_Ending.webp)

### Third ending: The silent end.

Do you remember this smiling man? He looks rather creepy, doesn't he? During our shift, we chose to serve him and offered him a shawarma. Then, this smiling man gave us a note. If you open the note at this point, it will trigger the third ending of the game.

You will be instantly transported to a bizarre and eerie alternate dimension. The sky is a deep green, and many blurry shapes float above. You will also pass by several gigantic and unsettling human body parts. After walking for a sufficient amount of time, a pair of massive jaws will suddenly snap shut, devouring you, and you will die, unlocking the "Silent Ending."

### Fourth ending: The erased ending.

"The Erased Ending" is another ending that requires breaking an important rule to unlock. This rule requires you not to look back at the shawarma stand during the inspection. The inspection event happens every day around 2 AM, without fail.

Around 2 AM, when the vehicle drives in and the edges of the screen turn red, you should immediately run to the back of the parking space and stay there until the lights return to normal. During the inspection, you shouldn't be aware of anything happening in the parking space.

If you choose to look back, you will be teleported to another world where you become incredibly small (or everything around you becomes incredibly large), and you'll find yourself moving down a conveyor belt. Just before you fall into a meat grinder, you will have a chance to look up and see a giant invisible creature wearing a suit and a hat, with bright lights for eyes, thus unlocking "The Erased Ending."

### Fifth ending: Neutralization ending

The last unlockable ending is the "Employee Neutralization" ending, which requires you to make five mistakes during your shift to unlock. If you make a mistake, such as being attacked by the meat, refusing a human customer, or serving an abnormal creature, a large piece of meat will appear between the refrigerator and the meat pile. **That's the Inspector.**

Each time you make a mistake, the Inspector will crawl out of the floor a little more, until his entire body is above ground and inside the kiosk. Once he's fully above ground, you're doomed. He will kill you if you get close to him, likely scaring you in the process, and unlocking the "Neutral Ending."

The best way to avoid this is to make sure you verify every customer's identity through the security cameras and check the footage from all angles. Keeping a close eye on your meat is also crucial. It's not always obvious when it will attack, and it might be too late before you realize it!
